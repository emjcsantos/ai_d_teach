# Code Of Conduct

AI D Teach welcomes thoughtful, respectful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, constructive, and patient.
- Assume good intent while asking clear questions.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Be especially careful with topics involving children, learning needs, privacy, and safety.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private data, API keys, child information, or sensitive screenshots.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors for unpaid urgent work.
- Introducing unsafe behavior such as hidden data collection or always-on recording.

## Reporting

For security issues, follow `SECURITY.md`. For conduct concerns, contact the maintainer through GitHub. Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participation when needed to keep the project safe and useful.
